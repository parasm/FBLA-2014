About Us


Choral Reef is a revolutionary new group that has recently entered the realm of producing. With inspiration from world-class DJs such as Swedish House Mafia and Skrillex, Choral Reef has goals to be on top of the charts everywhere. Choral Reef aims for quality entertainment towards all music lovers ranging from Kanye West fans to the creators of the Teen Titans original theme song. Choral Reef is currently on their Spring "The Reef is Getting Warmer" Tour in celebration of the release of the Enter The Reef EP. Tour guests include Skrillex, Stardust, and some tour dates even include the great Valx DJ. Welcome to the reef.
